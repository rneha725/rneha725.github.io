# On Writing

Writing is how I figure out what I actually think. If I can't write it clearly, I probably don't understand it clearly.

This isn't a writing blog. It's just a record of me trying to think better by putting things into words.

## Rules I'm trying to follow

1. Write every day, even if it's one sentence
2. Don't edit while drafting — drafting and editing are different mental modes
3. Read it out loud before publishing anything
4. Prefer short sentences. Then vary them occasionally, like this one which goes on a bit longer to break the rhythm.

## What I've noticed

The first paragraph is always the worst. The second is usually where the real thought starts. I've started deleting my first paragraphs more often.

**The goal isn't beautiful prose.** The goal is clear thinking that happens to be readable.
