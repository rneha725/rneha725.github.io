# Art Log

Not a portfolio. Just a log of things I made and what I learned from them. The bar is low on purpose.

## March 2026

- Tried stippling for the first time — incredibly meditative, will do again
- Experimented with watercolor on toned paper (the paper matters a lot)
- Started a portrait series. Abandoned it. Learned something from both halves.

## February 2026

- Charcoal figure sketches from online references
- First attempt at perspective drawing — buildings are hard
- Made a small linocut stamp (a leaf shape, very wobbly)

## What I want to try next

- Gouache — apparently more forgiving than watercolor
- Procreate on iPad, specifically for quick idea sketches
- Life drawing sessions if I can find one nearby

## The rule

One piece a month, no matter how bad. The point is the practice, not the product.
