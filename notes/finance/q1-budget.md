# Q1 2026 Budget

Tracking where money goes and making sure savings happen before spending, not after.

## Philosophy

Pay yourself first. Automate it. Then spend what's left without guilt.

## Q1 Goals

- [ ] Emergency fund: reach 3 months of expenses
- [ ] Start investing consistently, even if amounts are small
- [ ] Audit subscriptions — cancel anything unused for 30+ days
- [ ] Track every expense for at least 60 days to find patterns
